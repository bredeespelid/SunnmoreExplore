Your companion to Sunnmøre Experiences

Sunnmøre Explore is run by Hilde Maria Nicolaisen. Hilde has a great passion for Sunnmøre and wishes to share the joy of experiencing the region's beautiful nature – from mountains and fjords to local culture.

We focus on creating personalized experiences, not just standard tours. We aim to combine safe and inspiring activities like mountain hiking, climbing, and kayaking with cultural elements such as dance courses and lectures, if desired.

Whether you seek excitement in the mountains, tranquility by the fjord, or wish to learn more about Sunnmøre culture, Hilde will do her best to arrange a wonderful and memorable experience for you.

Our Services: Fjord, Mountain & Culture

Climbing Courses

Learn to climb safely outdoors or indoors in Sunnmøre. We offer everything from introductory to lead climbing (Brattkort) courses.

Mountain Hikes

Join a tailored mountain hike in Sunnmøre! We adapt tours in the Sunnmøre Alps to your preferences, from easy walks to challenging summit climbs.

Fjord Experiences

Experience the beautiful Sunnmøre fjords. We can arrange kayak tours and other adventures by the fjord.

Culture & Teambuilding

Interested in local culture? We can offer dance courses, choir workshops, lectures, and team building activities that combine nature and culture.
